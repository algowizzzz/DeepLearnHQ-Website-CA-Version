RC Leading Edge and DeepLearnHQ Collaborate to Build NFT Game

Mar 8, 2022

RC Leading Edge partners with DeepLearnHQ to build an NFT-powered game, leveraging blockchain technology for digital asset ownership.

RC Leading Edge and DeepLearnHQ have announced a collaboration to develop an NFT-powered game that integrates blockchain technology with engaging gameplay mechanics. The partnership combines RC Leading Edge's game design vision with DeepLearnHQ's proven expertise in blockchain and game development.

The game will feature NFT-based characters, items, and environments, allowing players to truly own their in-game assets. The development team is focused on creating a fun, accessible game that also delivers value through its blockchain integration.

This project is part of DeepLearnHQ's growing portfolio in the Web3 gaming space, where the company has become a trusted development partner.
