FAQ

Questions, answered.

How much does an MVP cost?

Mobile MVPs range from $15K-$50K. A focused MVP with core features starts at $15K; more complex apps with payments and real-time features start at $30K.

React Native or Flutter?

Both are excellent. React Native if your team knows JavaScript; Flutter for pixel-perfect UI. We help you choose based on your product needs.

Can you help with App Store submission?

Yes. We handle the full submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play, including compliance, screenshots, and descriptions.

What about post-launch iterations?

We build analytics in from day one. After launch, we help you analyze user behavior and iterate based on real data.
