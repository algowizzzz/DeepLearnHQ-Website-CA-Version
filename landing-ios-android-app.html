Native or cross-platform?

Cross-platform (React Native/Flutter) is ideal for 90% of apps. We recommend native Swift/Kotlin only for hardware-intensive apps like AR or games.

How long to build a mobile app?

Simple apps: 8-12 weeks. Feature-rich apps: 12-20 weeks. We ship working builds every 2 weeks.

Do you handle UI/UX design?

Yes. Our design process includes user research, wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and interactive prototypes — all before writing code.
