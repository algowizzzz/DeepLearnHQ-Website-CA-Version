DeepLearnHQ Is Now a Member of the Metaverse Standards Forum

Jul 20, 2022

DeepLearnHQ has joined the Metaverse Standards Forum, contributing to the development of open standards for the metaverse.

DeepLearnHQ has officially become a member of the Metaverse Standards Forum, joining an industry-wide initiative to foster the development of open standards for the metaverse. The forum brings together leading technology companies to collaborate on interoperability standards for metaverse experiences.

As a member, DeepLearnHQ will contribute its expertise in blockchain, game development, and immersive technologies to help shape the standards that will govern the future of the metaverse. The company's experience in building Web3 and gaming applications positions it well to contribute meaningfully to these efforts.

Joining the Metaverse Standards Forum reflects DeepLearnHQ's commitment to being at the forefront of emerging technology trends.
