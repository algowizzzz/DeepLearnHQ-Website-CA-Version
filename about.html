The Story

from day one.

The founding belief

We started with a simple observation: AI wasn't a buzzword in our office — it was a tool. Our founders knew the future of software wasn't about adding AI features; it was about building with AI as a core material, the way you'd build with steel or code.

AI-native from day one

There's a difference between a company that uses AI and one built for it. We've never known anything else. Our culture, hiring, and process are shaped around the principle that intelligence amplifies human judgment — we don't replace engineers with algorithms, we make engineers smarter.

Education is our commitment

Technology doesn't matter if people don't understand it. That's why we published 8 AI courses on Udemy. Over 35,000 learners globally now learn through our bilingual content. It's not a side project — it's core to who we are.

Where we are today

A founder-led, engineering-driven studio with 50+ shipped projects and teams across Madison and Toronto. We're not the largest agency — we're the one that stays hands-on, ships things that work, and genuinely cares about outcomes.

Our Principles

Eight things we stand for.

01

AI should serve people, not replace them.

The fear narrative is wrong. AI isn't coming to take your job — it's coming to change what your job is. We build systems that amplify human capability and keep decisions explainable, reversible, and answerable to humans.

02

Software should think, adapt, and improve.

Static software is dead software. The best systems learn from use, adapt to new data, and get smarter over time. We build living systems, not monuments.

03

Knowledge should be accessible to everyone.

Gatekeeping is a business model, not a principle. We teach what we build, publish what we learn, and make bilingual courses because language shouldn't be a barrier to opportunity.

04

Ship fast and learn faster.

Perfection is the enemy of learning. Build an MVP in a sprint, ship to real users, measure what matters, change when data says change. We'd rather ship good enough today and great tomorrow.

05

Small teams move faster than hierarchies.

A 5-person team that talks every day outweighs a 50-person team with org charts and approval chains. Our teams are small, senior, and autonomous.

06

Global talent changes the game.

The best engineer for your problem might live in Toronto, Madison, or São Paulo. Geography doesn't determine capability. We hire for skill, not proximity.

07

Partnerships trump vendor relationships.

We don't want clients — we want partners. We share risk, care about your long-term success, and say no when a project isn't a good fit. A partnership is aligned, not transactional.

08

We believe in building, not talking.

Manifestos are easy. Following through is hard. What matters is what you see when you work with us — launched products, real learners, genuine partnerships.
