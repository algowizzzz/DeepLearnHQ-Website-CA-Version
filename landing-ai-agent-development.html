FAQ

Questions, answered.

What's the difference between a chatbot and an AI agent?

Chatbots respond to messages. Agents take action — they reason through multi-step tasks, call APIs, make decisions, and complete workflows autonomously.

How long to build a custom AI agent?

8-14 weeks depending on complexity. Simple single-task agents ship in 6 weeks; multi-agent systems with tool orchestration take longer.

What does an AI agent cost?

Agent development ranges from $20K-$80K. Cost depends on the number of tools, integrations, and reasoning complexity required.

Is it safe to let AI agents take actions?

We build configurable guardrails, approval workflows, and full audit logs. You control what agents can do autonomously vs. what requires human approval.
