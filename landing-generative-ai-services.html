FAQ

Questions, answered.

What's the difference between ChatGPT and a custom LLM solution?

ChatGPT is a general tool. A custom solution uses your data, follows your business rules, and integrates with your systems — with guardrails against hallucination.

What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation connects an LLM to your data sources so it answers questions based on your actual documents, not its training data.

How much does generative AI integration cost?

Simple ChatGPT integrations start at $10K. Full RAG systems with custom models range from $25K-$80K.

How do you prevent hallucinations?

RAG with source citations, constrained outputs, confidence scoring, and human-review workflows for high-stakes decisions.
