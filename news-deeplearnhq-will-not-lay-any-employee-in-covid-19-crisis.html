DeepLearnHQ Will Not Lay Off Any Employee in COVID-19 Crisis

Apr 1, 2020

DeepLearnHQ announces its commitment to retaining all employees during the COVID-19 pandemic, prioritizing team welfare and job security.

In a bold and compassionate move during unprecedented times, DeepLearnHQ has announced that it will not lay off any employees during the COVID-19 crisis. The company's leadership has made a firm commitment to job security for its entire workforce, recognizing that its people are its most valuable asset.

CEO Saad Ahmed stated that the company has implemented comprehensive remote work protocols to ensure business continuity while prioritizing the health and safety of all team members. DeepLearnHQ has also introduced flexible working hours and additional support programs to help employees manage the challenges of working during a global pandemic.

This decision reflects DeepLearnHQ's core values of loyalty, integrity, and employee welfare. While many technology companies around the world have resorted to layoffs and furloughs, DeepLearnHQ has chosen to invest in its team, believing that this approach will strengthen the company in the long run.
