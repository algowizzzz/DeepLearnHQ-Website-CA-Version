DeepLearnHQ University to Go Ahead with Its Premier Internship Program 2021 with All COVID-19 Precautions

May 30, 2021

DeepLearnHQ University announces its 2021 internship program will proceed with comprehensive COVID-19 safety precautions in place.

DeepLearnHQ University has announced that its premier internship program for 2021 will go ahead with all necessary COVID-19 precautions in place. The program will follow a hybrid format combining in-person and remote learning to ensure the safety of all participants.

The internship program provides aspiring developers and designers with hands-on experience in software development, working alongside experienced professionals on real client projects. Participants will gain exposure to mobile development, web technologies, and emerging tech areas.

DeepLearnHQ remains committed to nurturing tech talent even during challenging times, and has implemented comprehensive health and safety protocols to protect interns and staff.
